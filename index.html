Daft Punk

Random Access Memories

  Their 4th album is called random access memories,this is also their last 
  album. This album was recorded in 5 studios (Henson Recording,conway Recording,
  Capitol Studios,Electric Lady, and Gang recording Studio) after the recording
  they released the album in 2013. Their album is 74:24 minutes long, the album
  has these songs in it:
  • Give Life Back to Music-fast strong
  • The Game of Love-slow
  • Giorgio by Moroder
  • Within-back to slow with piano
  • Instant Crush-up beat with nice melody
  • Lose Yourself to Dance-up beat disco
  • Touch-slow and long
  • Get Lucky-back to up-beat disco
  • Beyond-slow and melodic
  • Motherboard-slow and funky
  • Fragments of Time-up-beat background slow melody
  • Doin' it Right-up beat fast
  • Contact-slow and closing song
